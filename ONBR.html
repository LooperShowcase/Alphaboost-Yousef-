Fnaf (Five nights at freddy's)

Five Nights at Freddy's is an indie point-and-click horror game made by Scott Cawthon, which was first released on August 8th, 2014. The game has since spawned multiple sequels and several book series, a live action movie with sequels currently in production. The Five Nights at Freddy's Wiki aims to document all aspects of the games, books, and a movie. There are 4 main characters(for the first game fnaf 1 and for some time the others)
  • Freddy
  • Bonnie
  • Chica
  • Foxy
